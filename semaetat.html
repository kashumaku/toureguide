It is a must to see attraction in bahir dar to look in to the history of amhara and Ethiopia. the art galary is a must visit place. The monument, a spiral on a platform with some sculptures, on the East bank of the Blue Nile river commemorates those who died in a struggle in the 1970s and 80s to get the present regime in power. To enter the compound, guarded by military, foreigners have to pay 50 Birr. It is probably one of the few memorials in the world for which you have to pay to approach. the memorial monument locally known as semaetat.
